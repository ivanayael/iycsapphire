Ivana Yael Currá

@ivanayaelcurra | Lomas de Zamora, Buenos Aires, Argentina

Actress, Writer and Technology and Design Teacher. Dreaming of being a global actress and director of an Arts Academy

Hello, I'm Ivana Yael Currá, a passionate storyteller with a love for both the written word and the stage. I discover this just a year ago. As a writer, I weave tales that captivate and inspire, drawing upon my diverse background of anime, manga, novels, faitytales and a strong knowleage of CLAMP writing methodology and extremely vivid imagination helps me to create engaging narratives that resonate with audiences. Whether crafting compelling characters or exploring complex themes, I strive to infuse each piece with authenticity and depth. Even if you don't know where to start!

Alongside my writing journey, I've also pursued my dreams as an actress, bringing characters to life on stage and screen. With a natural flair for performance and a dedication to my craft, I immerse myself fully in every role, seeking to embody the essence of each character and convey their emotions with sincerity and conviction. I also like to invest myself into getting the best costume and design, perspective, photography, background and psychology of the characters to make them infuse with the story. Make them unique and part of the world they exist, like if where a living things, animals or humans

Driven by a relentless passion for storytelling and a commitment to artistic excellence, I am constantly seeking new opportunities to expand my creative horizons and make a meaningful impact in the world of literature and performing arts. Whether it's through the written word or the magic of performance, I aspire to touch hearts, provoke thought, and ignite imagination. I want to bring a sum of ideas part of this world, make them know and rich new scales

that's what stories are for, to be tell, be part of the memory in history and continue in the hand of anothers!
